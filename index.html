My Story

I did not come from a wealthy family. I live with my parents and three siblings in a small, decent, and rusty-roofed house. When it rains outside, it also rains inside. It sits beside a river-like body of water, so when the water level rises, a problem arises. I may not have the best house in town, but I have a God-fearing family, which is more important than anything. My father used to be a fisherman, but the pandemic hugely affected his job. My mother is a domestic helper, but due to some problems, she came back home. Despite the fact that we are not that fortunate in terms of wealth, I am very grateful that God gave me parents like them. They will sacrifice themselves for their children. Love, indeed.
